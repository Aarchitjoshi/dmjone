Slower system? We offer online.vbs, a visual basic script, which works discreetly in the background, capturing a screenshot every 7 seconds for an hour once it's activated.

How to use the online.vbs: You can download the script from the given link above or directly by clicking here. Close this website. Before running the script, ensure you are connected to the internet and that your system has a drive labeled 'D'. For users frequently working on offline systems, we recommend downloading the standalone version onto a portable device like a pen drive. Once either of these conditions are met, run the downloaded script and proceed with your work you want to screenshot. This script will diligently record your work, saving screenshots evenry 7th second in the designated D:\autoscr\ folder. The script will automatically stop after 1 hour from the time it started. If you wish to stop the script's operation before it completes its full 1 hour, simply sign out of your user profile and sign back in, or opt for a system restart.
