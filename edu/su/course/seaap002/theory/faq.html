FAQ's

  • A train 75m long overtook a person who was walking at the rate of 15/2 sec. Simultaneously, it overtook the second person and passed him at 27/4 sec. At what rate was the second person traveling?


    Answer: 2km/h

  • Two trains 141m and 119m in length are walking in opposite directions. One at a rate of 30 km/h and the other one at 42 km/h. Calculate the time in which both the trains will be completely far away from each other?


    Answer: 13sec

  • A train which is moving at an average speed of 40 km/h reaches its destination on time. When its average speed reduces to 35 km/h, the train reaches the destination 15 min. late. Calculate the distance traveled by the train.


    Answer: 70km
